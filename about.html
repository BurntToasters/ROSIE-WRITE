About ROSIE-WRITE

ROSIE-WRITE is a lightweight, browser-based note-taking application designed for simplicity and ease of use. It runs entirely in your browser and saves all your notes locally within your browser's local storage.

Features

  • Clean, modern interface.
  • Simple format options for quick note taking.
  • Autosave - Peace of mind with auto save constanly saving your work in the background.

Privacy First

Your notes are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage. This means:

  • Your note data never leaves your computer
  • No tracking or analytics. Your content is your content. Simple as that.
  • Open source! Rosie Write is open source. Check out the source code here on github.

More Info

ROSIE NOTES uses Cloudflare to easily deliver the web app and cache content, decreasing load on the origin. Cloudflare analytics are also in use to preserve user privacy while also allowing us to see the amount of visitors to the site.

Getting Started

Using ROSIE-WRITE is simple - just start typing! Your notes are automatically saved as you write. You can create multiple notes, format your text with the toolbar buttons, and export your work when you're ready to share it.
